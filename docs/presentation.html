Shift-share construction

Step 1 — baseline destination weights (2001 census)

\[ \begin{aligned} \omega_{md,0} &= \tfrac{N_{md,\,2001}}{\mathrm{Pop}_{m,\,2001}}, & \mathrm{mig\_int}_{m,0} &= \textstyle\sum_d \omega_{md,0}, & s_{md,0} &= \tfrac{\omega_{md,0}}{\mathrm{mig\_int}_{m,0}} \end{aligned} \]

$N_{md,2001}$ = migrants from muni $m$ to destination $d$ in 2001. $s_{md,0}$ are destination shares (sum to 1 within muni).

Step 2 — destination-country shifter (annual)

\[ \mathrm{fxshock}_{dt} \;=\; \frac{\mathrm{FX}_{dt}}{\mathrm{FX}_{d,\,2001}} \]

FX index of destination $d$'s currency vs NPR, indexed to 2001. Source: IMF FX panel, annual.

Step 3 — muni-year share-weighted shock

\[ \mathrm{fxshock}_{mt} \;=\; \sum_d s_{md,0}\,\mathrm{fxshock}_{dt} \]

Combines the shifter (destination FX, common across all Nepali munis) with the shares (each muni's 2001 destination mix). Standardised on the working sample before estimation.

Specification

\[ \begin{aligned} y_{it} \;=\;& \underbrace{\beta\bigl[\mathrm{fx}_z \cdot \log(\mathrm{mig\_int}_z)\bigr]}_{\text{migration-intensity-scaled FX shock (reported)}} \;+\; \underbrace{\boldsymbol{\lambda}_1'\bigl(\mathrm{mig\_int}_z\,\boldsymbol{\tau}_t\bigr)}_{C_{\text{mig}}\,:\,\text{year}\times\text{mig-intensity trend}} \\[14pt] & +\; \underbrace{\boldsymbol{\lambda}_2'\bigl(\mathrm{fx}_z\,\boldsymbol{\tau}_t\bigr)}_{C_{\text{fx}}\,:\,\text{year}\times\text{fx trend}} \;+\; \underbrace{\boldsymbol{\delta}'\bigl(\mathbf{X}_{m(i),0}\,\boldsymbol{\tau}_t\bigr)}_{C_X\,:\,\text{year}\times\text{destination-country characteristics}} \\[14pt] & +\; \alpha_m \;+\; \gamma_t \;+\; \varepsilon_{it} \end{aligned} \]

$\mathbf{X}_{m(i),0}$ = 2001 destination-weighted characteristics: six destination-region migrant shares + destination-weighted GDP per capita. Entity FE $\alpha_m$: muni for census panel, HH for HRVS HH panel. SE clustered at the municipality.
