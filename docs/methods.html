Method

Construction of the FX-driven shift-share instrument and the reduced-form specification, following Khanna et al. (2026).

Shock construction

The instrument follows Khanna et al. (2026) for the Philippines, adapted for Nepal where individual migrant income is unobserved. We use migrant counts as exposure weights instead. Let $m$ index municipalities, $d$ destination countries, $t$ years.

Define the per-capita baseline migrant exposure:

\begin{equation} \omega_{md,0} = \frac{N_{md,2001}}{\mathrm{Pop}_{m,2001}} \qquad \mathrm{MI}_{m,0} = \sum_d \omega_{md,0} \qquad s_{md,0} = \frac{\omega_{md,0}}{\mathrm{MI}_{m,0}} \label{eq:weights} \end{equation}

where $\mathrm{MI}_{m,0}$ is the total per-capita baseline migration intensity and $s_{md,0}$ is the destination composition (sums to 1 within $m$). The destination-country shifter is the FX index relative to 2001:

\begin{equation} \mathrm{FXshock}_{dt} = \frac{\mathrm{FX}_{dt}}{\mathrm{FX}_{d,2001}} \label{eq:shifter} \end{equation}

From these we build three regressors:

\begin{align} \mathrm{SSIV}_{mt} &= \sum_d \omega_{md,0} \, \mathrm{FXshock}_{dt} \;=\; \mathrm{MI}_{m,0} \cdot \mathrm{ShareShock}_{mt}\\ \mathrm{ShareShock}_{mt}&= \sum_d s_{md,0} \, \mathrm{FXshock}_{dt}\\ \mathrm{AbsExp}_{mt} &= \sum_d N_{md,2001} \, \mathrm{FXshock}_{dt} \label{eq:regressors} \end{align}

The headline regressor is $\mathrm{SSIV}_{mt}$. The identity $\mathrm{SSIV} = \mathrm{MI} \cdot \mathrm{ShareShock}$ is enforced and verified in script/vars/instrument.R §8.

Reduced-form specification

The headline panel regression is

\begin{equation} y_{mt} \;=\; \alpha_m + \gamma_t + \beta\,\mathrm{SSIV}_{m,t-\ell} + \boldsymbol{\lambda}_1' \bigl(\mathrm{MI}_{m,0}\,\boldsymbol{\tau}_t\bigr) + \boldsymbol{\lambda}_2' \bigl(\mathrm{ShareShock}_{m,0}\,\boldsymbol{\tau}_t\bigr) + \varepsilon_{mt}, \label{eq:main} \end{equation}

where

  • $\alpha_m$ is a municipality fixed effect;
  • $\gamma_t$ is a year fixed effect;
  • $\boldsymbol{\tau}_t = (\mathbf{1}\{t=t_1\},\ldots,\mathbf{1}\{t=t_T\})'$ is the vector of year-period indicators;
  • $\ell\in\{0,1,2\}$ is the lag set in the explorer.

Standard errors are clustered at the municipality (lgcode) level — the unit of observation, following Khanna et al. (2026, p. 1561) who cluster at the province level (their unit of observation). The headline cell that matches the basic script/baseline_census.R spec is census / P2_ref2011 / lag0 / ssiv_index / mi.

Robustness dimensions

Panel and reference year

  • P2_ref2011: 2011 + 2021 only. Headline.
  • P3_ref2001: full panel (2001, 2011, 2021), anchored at the pre-shock baseline.
  • P3_ref2011: full panel anchored at 2011.

Shock timing

  • $\mathrm{FXshock}_t$: contemporaneous shifter.
  • $\mathrm{FXshock}_{t-1}$, $\mathrm{FXshock}_{t-2}$: lagged 1 / 2 years; addresses contemporaneity in the FX panel.

Shock variant

  • $\mathrm{SSIV}$ (level index): main spec.
  • $\mathrm{SSIV}$ winsorised at 99%: outlier-robust version that caps the SSIV at its 99th percentile to limit top-1% leverage.
