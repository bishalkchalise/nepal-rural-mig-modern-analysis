District-level robustness

Khanna shift-share IV, lag-2, district panel

Bishal K. Chalise · Mushfiq Mobarak

Three panels — census 2011/2021 (77 dist. × 2 yrs), HRVS 2016-18 (residualised to district × year), and NEC 2018 firm cross-section (77 dist.). Each table walks the Khanna control ladder.

← main presentation

Specification

$y_{d,t} = \beta\, z\bigl(\log(\mathrm{mig}_{d,t-2}/1000)\bigr) + \boldsymbol{\delta}'\mathbf{C}_{d,t} + \alpha_d + \tau_t + \varepsilon_{d,t}$

  • $z(\log(\mathrm{mig}/1000))$ instrumented by 2009-10 DOFE-share × year shifter (26 destinations).
  • Lag 2 throughout. SE clustered at district.
  • Three controls compared per table:
    • + $C_{\mathrm{mig}}$ (M2): year × baseline log(mig) interaction.
    • + $C_{\mathrm{fx}}$ (M3): M2 + bare $z$ (FX-anchored shifter).
    • + $C_{X}$ (M4): M3 + year × 6 region shares.

Dependent variable scaling: log of continuous outcomes, level for indicator / share outcomes. Mean(Y) reported in the original (level) scale.
