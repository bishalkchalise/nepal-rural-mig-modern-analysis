Summary statistics

Mean, standard deviation, and N of every outcome variable across the four underlying datasets. Each dataset reports its own year structure.
Notes. Each cell reports mean (sd) [N] of the outcome at the dataset's native unit-of-observation. Census cells are cross-municipality (743 munis in 2001, 753 in 2011 and 2021; 2021 is a 7.5% sample). HRVS cells are household-year. NEC firm cells are cross-municipality at the 2018 firm census. NEC firm-panel cells are pooled muni × founding-year, binned into three cohort windows. Empty cells (—) indicate the variable was not measured in that dataset/year.
