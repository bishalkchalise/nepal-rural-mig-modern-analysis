Estimation equation — what changes across the nine specifications

Every spec estimates the same regression form. Only the treatment term and the year × mig_int_z control vary:

yi,t  =  β · TREATMENTm(i),t  +  λ1 · [ year × mig_int_z ]  +  λ2 · [ year × fx_z ]  +  δ · [ year × Block A ]  +  αm  +  γt  +  εi,t

Common across all 9 specs. year × fx_z and Block A × year always included. Entity FE αm is muni for census + NEC panel, HH for HRVS, district for NEC 2018 cs. SE clustered at municipality (district for NEC cs). Sample: muni-baseline migrants ≥ k.

Spec TREATMENTm,t year × mig_int_z form What's tested
S0_baseline fxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z (linear) Anchor — literature-standard SSIV. Mixed log/linear forms on the two sides deliberately decouple treatment from c_mig.
S_lag1fxz,m,t−1 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zFX shifter lagged 1 year.
S_lag2fxz,m,t−2 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 2 years.
S_lag3fxz,m,t−3 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 3 years.
S_lag4fxz,m,t−4 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 4 years.
S_lag5fxz,m,t−5 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 5 years.
S_lag10fxz,m,t−10 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 10 years — loses census 2001 cohort.
S_both_log fxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × log(mig_int_z) "Consistent log" — same functional form on both sides; near-collinear with treatment on long panels.
S_both_linear fxz,m,t · mig_int_zm (linear share) year × mig_int_z "Consistent linear" — linear share inside treatment to match the linear c_mig.

Standardisation: fx_z, mig_int_z, and log_mig_int_z are z-scored within each working sample (k threshold) before estimation. Block A = six 2001 destination-region migrant shares + destination-weighted GDP per capita, each interacted with year dummies. Coefficient (β) stars: * p<0.10, ** p<0.05, *** p<0.01.
