Estimation equation — what changes across the nine specs

Every spec estimates the same regression form. Only the treatment term and the year × mig_int_z control vary:

yi,t  =  β · TREATMENTm(i),t  +  λ1 · [ year × mig_int_z ]  +  λ2 · [ year × fx_z ]  +  δ · [ year × Block A ]  +  αm  +  γt  +  εi,t
SpecTREATMENTyear × mig_int_z formWhat's tested
S0_baselinefxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z (linear) Anchor — literature-standard SSIV with mixed log/linear forms across treatment vs c_mig.
S_lag1..S_lag5fxz,m,t−L · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zFX shifter lagged 1–5 years (delayed-response tests).
S_lag10fxz,m,t−10 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_zLagged 10 years (loses census 2001 cohort).
S_both_logfxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × log(mig_int_z) "Consistent log" — same functional form on both sides; near-collinear on long panels.
S_both_linearfxz,m,t · mig_int_zm (linear share) year × mig_int_z"Consistent linear" — linear share inside treatment to match the linear c_mig.

Common across all 9 specs: year × fx_z, Block A × year, entity FE αm (muni for census + NEC panel, HH for HRVS, district for NEC cs), year FE γt, SE clustered at municipality (district for NEC cs). Treatments z-scored on the working sample.
