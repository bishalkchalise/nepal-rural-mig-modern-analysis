The estimation equation — what changes across the nine specs

Every spec estimates the same regression form. Only the red treatment term and the blue year × mig_int_z control change:

yi,t  =  β · TREATMENTm(i),t  +  λ1 · [ year × mig_int_z ]  +  λ2 · [ year × fx_z ]  +  δ · [ year × Block A ]  +  αm  +  γt  +  εi,t

Common across all 9 specs: year × fxz control, Block A × year (year-interacted destination-region shares + destination GDP), entity FE αm (muni for census + NEC panel; HH for HRVS; district for NEC 2018 cs), year FE γt, SE clustered at municipality (district for NEC cs). Sample: k ≥ baseline-migrant threshold (default k=25).

Spec TREATMENTm,t year × mig_int_z form What's being tested
S0_baseline fxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z (linear) Anchor — literature-standard SSIV. Mixed log/linear forms across treatment vs control deliberately decouple them to preserve identifying variation.
S_lag1 fxz,m,t−1 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 1 year — tests delayed responses.
S_lag2 fxz,m,t−2 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 2 years.
S_lag3 fxz,m,t−3 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 3 years.
S_lag4 fxz,m,t−4 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 4 years.
S_lag5 fxz,m,t−5 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 5 years.
S_lag10 fxz,m,t−10 · log(mig_int_zm) year × mig_int_z FX shifter lagged 10 years — loses census 2001 cohort.
S_both_log fxz,m,t · log(mig_int_zm) year × log(mig_int_z) "Consistent log" — same functional form on both sides. Near-collinear with treatment on long panels (NEC); use as a diagnostic, not an identifying spec.
S_both_linear fxz,m,t · mig_int_zm (linear share) year × mig_int_z "Consistent linear" — linear share inside treatment to match the linear c_mig control. Also produces over-control on long panels.

Standardisation: fx_z and mig_int_z / log_mig_int_z are all z-scored within each working sample (k threshold) before estimation. Block A consists of six 2001 destination-region migrant shares plus destination-weighted GDP per capita, each interacted with year dummies. Coefficient (β) is reported with stars: * p<0.10, ** p<0.05, *** p<0.01.
